Target App Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the Target App (hereby referred to as the "Application") developed by Prem M & Mohamed Riaz (hereby referred to as the "Service Provider"). Target App is a free file-sharing application that enables users to transfer files securely using NFC and mic/speaker technology.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • File Transfer Logs: Basic metadata such as file names, sizes, transfer time, and device identifiers may be logged locally on your device to help with transfer history and troubleshooting.
  • Device Information: We may collect anonymized data about your device model, operating system, and connection type to optimize performance.
  • Location (optional): With your permission, approximate location data may be used for device discovery (e.g., Nearby/NFC sharing).

How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Enable File Sharing: To facilitate the secure and smooth sharing of files between nearby devices.
  • Improve App Functionality: To monitor performance, detect bugs, and optimize user experience.
  • Security and Abuse Prevention: To detect unauthorized or abusive activity and protect user data.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. However, we may share information under the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena, court order, or similar legal process.
  • Security Threats: When necessary to prevent potential threats to personal safety or violations of our Terms of Use.

Data Retention

We retain local logs and transfer history only as long as necessary for app functionality. You may delete this data manually from within the app settings. We do not store files or personal data on external servers.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: You can view or export local file transfer history.
  • Deletion: You may delete all local app data at any time from your device settings.
  • Permissions: You can revoke location or microphone access at any time from system settings.

Security

We are committed to securing your data. Measures include:

  • Local-Only Storage: We do not upload files or logs to the cloud.
  • Permission-Based Access: The app requests only essential permissions for its core features (e.g., mic, NFC).
  • Encrypted Transfers: When possible, files are transferred securely using encrypted communication protocols.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Your continued use of the app implies your acceptance of the revised policy.
